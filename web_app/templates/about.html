Our Story

CleanSlate was inspired by a need for greater control over our social media data. With so many platforms storing personal posts and messages, we realized there was no easy way to manage and delete this data across different platforms. Our journey started as a project to solve this issue.

We wanted to create a user-friendly, open-source tool that empowers users to manage their social presence on their terms.
